Hi, check out my plants!

These are time series graphs displaying the watering status of my chili plants over the last two days.
The Y-axis is kind of arbitrary; it displays the raw ADC value from my sensor.
For the mystery chili this means that higher is drier, and for the habanero lower is drier.
The boards are hooked up differently, this is a work in progress.

For reference, between 400 and 600 is a healthy range, and 100 outside of these my plants are hurting :c
