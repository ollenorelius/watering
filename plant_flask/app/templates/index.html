Hi, check out my plants!

These are time series graphs displaying the watering status of my chili plants over the last two days.
The Y-axis is kind of arbitrary; it displays the raw ADC value from my sensors.

For reference, between 400 and 750 is a healthy range, and 100 outside of these my plants are hurting :c
The plants should get automatically watered at 450, assuming there is a pump connected to that plant, and that there's water in the tank.

In short, the system stack is MQTT -> Prometheus -> Grafana -> Flask.
